Rules

The purpose of this memory game is to memorize the locations of the cards in the game and to make pairs of cards by turning them over 2 by 2 in the least amount of moves and in the shortest time.

When the 2 cards match, it's a pair! You win the pair and have the right to play again, otherwise the cards are automatically turned faced down and you have to make a new try.
